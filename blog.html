Mapping Scooter Injuries in Lexington

Published on July 14, 2025

This post highlights a recent interactive map I built using Leaflet.js and clustering techniques to analyze motorized scooter accidents in Lexington, Kentucky.

The data comes from the Lexington-Fayette County Police Department and includes incident reports from 2019 to 2022. My aim was to build an exploratory web tool that helps residents, planners, and policymakers understand spatial patterns in e-scooter injuries and how they vary over time.

The map supports clustering for dense areas, a time filter, and interactive popups showing incident details.
